## Products & Materials

**Q: What's the difference between Road Crush and Pit Run?**
A: Road Crush is screened, sized, and engineered for road base. Pit Run is mixed aggregate from quarry operations, suitable for fill and base with proper compaction. Road Crush is higher quality but more costly.

**Q: Which material is best for driveways?**
A: Road Crush (1/2" to 2.5") works excellently for driveway base (4-6 inches). Top with Washed Rock for finished appearance. A 50-ton load covers approximately 200 square feet at 6 inches deep.

**Q: Can you help me calculate how much material I need?**
A: Absolutely! Call with your dimensions (length × width × depth in inches) and material type. We'll calculate tonnage and cost.

**Q: What's the best material for landscaping?**
A: Depends on application: Pea Gravel for paths, Decorative Stone for visual accents, Washed Rock for borders, Boulders for focal points. Topsoil works great for planting beds.

**Q: Are your materials environmentally sourced?**
A: Yes. We source responsibly and operate compliant quarries. Recycled aggregate is available - call for details.

**Q: Can I mix materials for custom blends?**
A: Yes! We can create custom blends. Minimum 10 tons. Discuss specifications with our team.

**Q: What's the best material for erosion control?**
A: Larger stone (Fractured Rock, River Rock) provides weight and stability. Drainage Rock supports proper water flow. Our team can recommend specific solutions based on site conditions.
