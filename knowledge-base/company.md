# WC-CON Company Profile

## About Westlake Crushing & Contracting (WC-CON)

Westlake Crushing & Contracting is a family-owned and operated aggregate supply, crushing, and trucking company based in Hinton, Alberta. With over 45 years of experience serving the Yellowhead Region and beyond, WC-CON has established itself as a reliable, professional partner for construction companies, municipalities, landscapers, and development projects throughout Alberta.

---

## Company History

### Founding & Early Years
- **Founded**: 1980
- **Location**: Hinton, Alberta
- **Initial Focus**: Small aggregate supply and local trucking operations
- **Founders**: Family visionaries who saw opportunity in the growing Yellowhead Region

### Growth & Expansion (1980s-2000s)
- **1985**: Added crushing capabilities with first mobile crusher
- **1990**: Expanded to equipment rental division
- **1995**: Service area expanded to Grande Cache and Edson
- **2000**: Modernized fleet with latest dump trucks and lowboys
- **2005**: Certified with COR (Certificate of Recognition) for safety excellence

### Modern Era (2010s-Present)
- **2010**: Achieved WCB Safety Recognition Award
- **2013**: Named Business of the Year (Yellowhead Region)
- **2015**: Expanded to 50+ employees, 40+ vehicles and equipment units
- **2017**: Second Business of the Year Award; earned ISNetworld compliance
- **2020**: Launched environmental initiatives; enhanced sustainability practices
- **2023**: Implemented digital ordering and logistics tracking
- **2024**: Expanding service to northern Alberta; planning First Nations partnerships

### 45+ Years of Excellence
Today, WC-CON operates a modern fleet and equipment inventory, maintains excellent safety standards, and continues the family tradition of reliable, customer-focused service.
