### Road Crush
- **Available Sizes**: 1/2"-2.5", 2", Custom sizes
- **Description**: Premium-grade crushed aggregate for road base, driveways, and construction sites
- **Uses**: Road construction, driveway base, subgrade preparation, load-bearing applications
- **Pricing**: Call for current rates (typically $16-22/ton depending on size and volume)

### Washed Rock
- **Available Sizes**: 1/2", 3/4", 1", 1.5"
- **Description**: Clean, washed aggregate suitable for decorative and functional applications
- **Uses**: Landscaping, river rock beds, drainage, decorative finishes
- **Pricing**: Competitive rates (typically $22-26/ton)

- **Pricing**: Call for quote

### Natural Round Rock
- **Sizes**: 1"-3"
- **Description**: Naturally rounded stone with aesthetic appeal
- **Uses**: Decorative landscaping, dry riverbeds, garden borders, water feature beds
- **Pricing**: Premium pricing (typically $20-24/ton)
- **Available**: Year-round, bulk delivery available

### Pea Gravel
- **Sizes**: Small rounded pebbles
- **Description**: Smooth, decorative gravel perfect for landscape projects
- **Uses**: Garden paths, playground surfaces, decorative mulch, perimeter landscaping
- **Pricing**: $24-28/ton
- **Delivery Options**: Bulk truck or bagged (25kg bags available)

### Drain Rock
- **Description**: Coarse aggregate for drainage applications
- **Uses**: French drains, drainage rock beds, septic system support, moisture management
- **Pricing**: Economical rates ($14-16/ton)
- **Key Benefit**: Essential for proper water management in construction

### Rainbow Rock
- **Description**: Colorful, multi-hued decorative stone
- **Uses**: Accent landscaping, garden decoration, pathway borders, visual enhancement
- **Pricing**: Premium decorative pricing
- **Available Finishes**: Natural (as-quarried), washed

### Pit Run
- **Description**: Mixed aggregate blend from quarry operations
- **Uses**: General fill, base layer preparation, road base (with compaction)
- **Pricing**: Most economical option ($13-15/ton)
- **Volume**: Best for large fill projects

### Sand Products
- **Fine Sand**: For concrete mixes, sandboxes, landscaping
- **Coarse Sand**: Drainage applications, concrete finishing
- **Mason Sand**: For masonry and mortar work
- **Pricing**: $18-22/ton depending on type
- **Uses**: Construction, concrete production, landscaping, filtration
