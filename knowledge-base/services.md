# WC-CON Services

## Trucking & Transportation

### Fleet Overview
WC-CON operates a modern fleet of specialized trucks to handle all material transportation and hauling needs.

### Tank Trucks
- **Capacity**: 5,000-10,000 gallons
- **Uses**: Water delivery, liquid transport, dust control
- **Service Area**: Full service area coverage
- **Availability**: Daily dispatch available

### Lowboys (Low Deck Trailers)
- **Capacity**: Up to 50 tons
- **Uses**: Heavy equipment transport, large machinery hauling, project delivery
- **Features**: Adjustable height, safety chains, professional operators
- **Available**: With experienced drivers for safe loading/unloading

### Winch Tractors
- **Power**: Heavy-duty pulling capacity
- **Uses**: Extraction, difficult access sites, pulling services
- **Equipment**: Professional winch operators on staff
- **Emergency Service**: Available for emergency towing/extraction

### Belly Dumps (Pup Trailers)
- **Capacity**: 15-18 tons per load
- **Advantages**: Quick unloading via pneumatic system, minimal site disturbance
- **Uses**: Material delivery with fast unload times
- **Efficiency**: Reduces on-site labor requirements

### Truck & Pup Combinations
- **Configuration**: Power unit with single or double pup trailers
- **Capacity**: 30-40 tons total per run
- **Advantages**: Maximize hauling with single-unit efficiency
- **Popular For**: High-volume material transport, gravel/aggregate delivery

### Body Jobs (Dump Trucks)
- **Capacity**: 12-20 tons per load
- **Operations**: Hydraulic lift for rapid unloading
- **Uses**: Aggregate delivery, site fill, material transport
- **Flexibility**: Can work on smaller job sites with tight access

### End Dumps
- **Capacity**: 18-25 tons per load
- **Unloading**: Rear hoist system for efficient delivery
- **Best For**: Linear projects (roads, trenches), high-volume material distribution
- **Coverage**: All standard routes within service area

### Specialized Services
- **Equipment Transport**: Excavators, loaders, dozers (lowboy rates)
- **Oversized Loads**: Permits and escort services arranged
- **After-Hours Delivery**: Evening and weekend service available
- **Project Coordination**: Dispatch team coordinates multi-truck operations
