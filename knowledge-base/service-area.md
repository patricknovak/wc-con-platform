# WC-CON Service Area

## Primary Service Territory

WC-CON is based in Hinton, Alberta and serves a primary market across the Yellowhead Region of Alberta. Our home base allows us to provide rapid, efficient service to surrounding communities and projects.

---

## Delivery Logistics

### Standard Delivery Windows
- **In-Town (Hinton)**: Same-day if ordered by 10 AM
- **Regional (Edson, Jasper, Robb)**: Next business day standard
- **Extended (Grande Cache, Cadomin)**: 2-3 days with notice
- **Remote Locations**: Custom timing; call for estimate

### Scheduling Coordination
- **Order Before 10 AM**: Same-day delivery possible for in-stock materials
- **24-Hour Notice**: Recommended for next-day delivery
- **1-2 Days Notice**: Standard for regional areas
- **1+ Week Notice**: Better rates for remote/large orders

### Bulk Order Considerations
- **Large Orders (100+ tons)**: May require multiple trucks; schedule in advance
- **Multiple Sites**: Can split loads across 2-3 locations
- **Phased Delivery**: Schedule staged deliveries for large projects
- **Fleet Coordination**: Dispatch teams multiple trucks for efficiency

### Weather & Seasonal Factors
- **Summer**: Best service window; minimal delays
- **Fall**: Good conditions; occasional early snow
- **Winter**: Normal service; pass conditions may add travel time
- **Spring**: Breakup period may restrict heavy loads; contact for status
- **Updates**: Call for current conditions if weather a concern

## Service Levels & Guarantees

### Standard Service Level
- **Reliability**: 99% on-time performance in normal conditions
- **Communication**: Dispatch updates during delivery window
- **Quality**: Material meets grade specifications; professional unloading

### Premium Service Options
- **Priority Delivery**: Guaranteed next-day delivery (call for premium)
- **Weekend Service**: Saturday delivery available (premium rates)
- **After-Hours**: Evening/night delivery options (emergency rates)
- **Operator Service**: Experienced crusher/equipment operators available

### Distance-Based Rates
- **0-50 km**: Standard delivery rate included in pricing
- **50-100 km**: Distance premium applies (~$0.15-0.25/km)
- **100-150 km**: Extended distance rates (~$0.20-0.35/km)
- **150+ km**: Custom quote required based on route/load
- **Volume Discounts**: May offset distance premiums for large orders

## Getting Service & Information

### Check Service Availability
- **Call Dispatch**: (780) 865-6000 for immediate availability
- **Ask Direct**: Questions about your specific location/project
- **Online Lookup**: Use postcode to check service area on website
- **Email Inquiry**: Submit location/project details via website form

### Service Request Process
1. **Call Dispatch**: Provide location, material type, quantity, delivery date
2. **Availability Confirmed**: Dispatcher checks stock and routing
3. **Quote Provided**: Rates based on material, distance, timing
4. **Booking Confirmed**: Schedule delivery with specific window
5. **Delivery Coordination**: Final details/updates day before
6. **On-Time Delivery**: Professional service at your location

### Special Arrangements
- **Remote Sites**: Pre-visit may be necessary for difficult access
- **Large Orders**: Logistics planning meeting recommended
- **Phased Deliveries**: Project manager assigned for coordination
- **Custom Routing**: Multiple site deliveries planned efficiently
